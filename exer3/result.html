Eight Sushi Lounge

Eight Sushi Lounge is located in 930 Howell Mill Rd NW, Atlanta, GA 30318. The Wagyu sando there is super good. Remember to make a reservation before visiting!

Ten Seconds Noodle

Ten Seconds Noodle is known for its Yunnan rice noodle, it is my first lunch choice. Huajiao Chicken Rice Noodle is my favorite. The snacks there are also great!

Friends chef restaurant

My friends are the best chef I know in Atlanta! Check out the Salt and pepper pork ribs, braised pork trotters, yellow sauce chicken etc.!
